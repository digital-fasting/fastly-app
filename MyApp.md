Reclaim your attention. Calm your mind. Fastly makes digital fasting effortless.

In a world of endless notifications, breaking news, and social media loops, mental overload has become the new normal. Fastly helps you take back control — one intentional fast at a time.

WHAT IS A DIGITAL FAST? A digital fast is a conscious decision to step away from a specific mental trigger — news, social media, gossip, arguments, shopping, sugar, or negativity — for a set amount of time. It's not about quitting forever. It's about choosing when you engage.

HOW FASTLY WORKS

Choose your trigger — Pick from built-in triggers or create your own custom ones
Set a duration — From 1 hour to a full week
Commit & go — Fastly tracks your progress and keeps you accountable
That's it. No complicated rituals. No rigid rules. Just you, a clear intention, and a countdown.

KEY FEATURES

• Live countdown timer — Always know how far you've come and how much is left
• Home Screen widget — Glance at your fast without even unlocking your phone
• Lock Screen widget — Your commitment, always in view
• Custom triggers — Name your own fasts and pick a personal icon & color
• Fast history & streaks — See your patterns and celebrate your wins
• Milestone celebrations — Confetti and badges when you hit key checkpoints
• Scheduled fasts — Plan fasts in advance so you never miss a beat
• Break tracking — If you break a fast, log the reason and restart stronger
• iCloud sync — Your fasts follow you across all your Apple devices
• Notifications — Gentle check-ins and completion alerts

INSIGHTS THAT MOTIVATE

Track your total fasting hours, longest streaks, most-used triggers, and completion rates. Fastly turns your discipline into visible data so you stay motivated and see real progress over time.
