# Fastly

**Your fasting companion.**

Step back from what drains you — news, social media, endless feeds — for an hour, a day, or a week. Set your intention, start a fast, and let Fastly keep you accountable with a live timer, Home Screen widget, and gentle check-ins.

**What you can do**
- Choose your trigger (built-in or your own)
- Set a duration from 1 hour to 1 week
- See your progress and streaks
- Add the widget and stay on track without opening the app

Reclaim your attention. Feel the difference.
